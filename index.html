Did you know?

The Akan people of Ghana frequently
give their children names in accordance
with the day of the week in which they were born.
The day names they are given have a
meaning that is concerned with the soul
and character of the person.
The middle names on the other hand,
have considerably more varieties and can
refer to their birth order, twin status
or an ancestor's middle name.
